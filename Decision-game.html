How to Play

You will see a picture on the left, a word on the right, and a phrase in the middle: IS or IS NOT.

  • If the statement is TRUE, press TRUE.
  • If the statement is FALSE, press FALSE.
  • IS means the word should match the picture for TRUE.
  • IS NOT means the word should NOT match the picture for TRUE.

Modes:

  • Practice – unlimited questions until you stop. No scores are saved.
  • Short – 10 questions. Score is recorded only if you finish all 10.
  • Long – 20 questions. Score is recorded only if you finish all 20.
  • Infinite – continues until your first mistake or timeout. Time allowed starts at 1 second and shrinks by 0.1 seconds every 50 questions down to 0.3s.

Enter your initials if you want your best Short, Long, or Infinite scores on the global leaderboard.
